Soul Land 2:The Peerless Tang Sect-Episode-5 A Hundred Thousand Year Soul Ring

Set several years after the events of the first Soul Land, the sequel follows Huo Yuhao, a young, seemingly talentless boy who forms a spirit contract with a million-year-old intelligent spirit beast, the Skydream Iceworm. This partnership grants him unique spiritual powers and sets him on the path to becoming a powerful soul master. He enrolls in the Shrek Academy to hone his skills, where he forms the Tang Sect's first soul tool department and builds deep bonds with his friends, especially his fated love, Wang Dong'er. The overarching story chronicles Huo Yuhao's incredible journey of growth, revenge, and protecting the continent from looming threats, all while uncovering the mysteries of Wang Dong'er's own divine lineage.
