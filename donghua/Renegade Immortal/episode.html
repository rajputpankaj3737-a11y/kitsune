The story is a profound and often tragic journey of his endless pursuit for power and life, all to protect his dwindling family and uncover the secrets of his own existence. It's a tale steeped in loneliness and sacrifice, where Wang Lin must constantly walk the path of a renegade, defying heaven, fate, and even his own heart to grasp a destiny that is truly his own.
